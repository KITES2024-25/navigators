About Us

Katha began its vocational training and skill development initiative in the 1990s as part of Katha SRI (Slum Resurgence Initiative) with the motto - 'when women earn, children learn'. We offered courses in tailoring and baking, which were taught at the Katha Lab School. Women were also organised into self-help groups to support them in establishing their own entrepreneurial ventures. This led to the establishment of the Katha School of Entrepreneurship, which has now expanded into providing digital training courses through KITES (Katha Information Technology and Entrepreneurship School). Through KITES, Katha has successfully trained over 37,000 beneficiaries, achieving a placement rate of approximately 82%.
